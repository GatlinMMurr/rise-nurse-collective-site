Privacy Policy

How Rise Nurse Collective collects, uses, and protects information on this website.

Information We Collect

When you contact us through this website, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message.

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, coordinate services, and communicate with you about Rise Nurse Collective. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Health Information

This website is not intended for transmitting detailed protected health information (PHI). Please do not include specific diagnoses, full medication lists, or other sensitive medical information in online forms. Once we connect, we will use more secure methods to exchange PHI.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use basic website analytics to understand how visitors use our site and to improve it over time. This may include general traffic, page views, and browser type.

Your Choices

If you wish to update or request deletion of information you’ve shared with us through this website, please contact us and we will make reasonable efforts to honor your request.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the most recent changes.
