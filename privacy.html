How we protect your information

We value your trust. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it secure.

Rise Nurse Collective follows best practices for confidentiality and recommends avoiding detailed medical information in online forms. Once connected, we will use more secure communication channels for protected health information.

Information We Collect

When you contact us through this website, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message.

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, coordinate services, and communicate with you about Rise Nurse Collective. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Health Information

This website is not intended for transmitting detailed protected health information (PHI). Please avoid sharing specific diagnoses, full medication lists, or other sensitive medical information in online forms. When appropriate, we will use secure methods to exchange PHI.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use basic website analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This typically includes general traffic patterns, page views, browser type, and similar non-identifying data.

Your Choices

If you wish to update or request deletion of information you’ve shared through this website, please contact us. We will make reasonable efforts to honor your request.
