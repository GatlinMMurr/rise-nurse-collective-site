Our Services

Comprehensive DDA Nurse Delegation and supportive nursing services tailored to individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities.

DDA Nurse Delegation

We provide ongoing Nurse Delegation services in accordance with Maryland regulations. Our RNs:

  • Assess individuals for delegable nursing tasks
  • Develop and update nursing care plans
  • Train and evaluate direct support staff competency
  • Provide ongoing supervision and re-delegation as needed
  • Document delegation in compliance with DDA and nursing board standards

Caregiver & Staff Training

Our nurses equip staff and caregivers with the skills and confidence they need to safely complete delegated nursing tasks. Training may include:

  • Medication administration and documentation
  • Health observation and when to notify the nurse
  • Safety, infection control, and emergency response
  • Person-centered approaches to health support

Future booking system will allow agencies to schedule classes online.

Health Consultation & Support

We collaborate with teams as health needs change. Consultation can support:

  • Review of new diagnoses and medications
  • Transition planning between settings
  • Care plan updates and risk mitigation
  • Team education around complex health needs
