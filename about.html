Rise Nurse Collective

Rooted in compassion and guided by regulation, Rise Nurse Collective supports people with intellectual and developmental disabilities through thoughtful, person-centered nursing.

We specialize in DDA Nurse Delegation—helping agencies, staff, and families feel confident that care is safe, compliant, and grounded in the person’s everyday life.

Who We Are

Rise Nurse Collective LLC is a nurse-led partner for agencies and teams serving people with intellectual and developmental disabilities in Maryland. We focus on practical, relationship-based nursing support—so complex regulations translate into clear, doable steps for staff and families.

Our work sits at the intersection of people’s real lives and regulatory requirements. We aim to make nursing support feel less intimidating and more like a natural part of the person’s daily routine.

Our Mission

To bridge compassionate nursing care with regulatory excellence—ensuring safe, person-centered support for every individual we serve.

Our Approach

We collaborate with direct support staff, families, and provider agencies so that nursing care is integrated, understandable, and aligned with the person’s goals.

What We Value

Dignity, clear communication, and shared responsibility. We believe good nursing support feels respectful, calm, and sustainable for the whole team.

Who We Serve

We partner with agencies and teams supporting people with intellectual and developmental disabilities, particularly within Maryland’s DDA-funded systems.

Residential & Supported Living Providers

We support homes of all sizes with training, delegation, and RN collaboration.

Day & Employment Programs

Ensuring safe, consistent care across daily routines and community settings.

Family Caregivers & Guardians

Clear communication and guidance for caregivers supporting loved ones at home.

Support Coordinators & IDT Members

Collaboration on plans, risk mitigation, and person-centered goals.
