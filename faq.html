Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our services, Nurse Delegation, and what to expect when working with us.

What is Nurse Delegation?

Nurse Delegation is a process where a registered nurse trains and authorizes unlicensed staff to safely perform specific nursing tasks, such as medication administration, in community settings.

Who is eligible for your services?

We primarily serve individuals receiving DDA-funded services, as well as the provider agencies and caregivers who support them. If you’re unsure whether you qualify, contact us and we’ll talk it through.

Do you offer classes or group trainings?

Yes. We provide staff trainings and will soon launch a booking system so agencies can schedule classes online. For now, please contact us directly to discuss training needs.

Is my information confidential?

We take privacy and confidentiality seriously and follow all applicable regulations. We recommend not sharing detailed medical information via the website form; once connected, we can exchange information using more secure methods.
